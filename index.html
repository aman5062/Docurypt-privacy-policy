Privacy Policy for Docurypt

Last Updated: March 2, 2026

Overview

Docurypt is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our app handles your data.

Data Collection

Docurypt collects ZERO data.

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, analytics, usage data, or any other data from our users.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:

  • Encrypted files: Stored in app's private data directory
  • Database: SQLite database stored locally on your device
  • No cloud storage: Nothing is uploaded to any server
  • No external servers: App never connects to any external service

Storage Locations

  • Android: /data/data/com.docurypt.app/
  • Linux: ~/.local/share/com.docurypt.app/
  • Windows: %APPDATA%\com.docurypt.app\
  • macOS: ~/Library/Application Support/com.docurypt.app/

Internet Connection

Docurypt never connects to the internet.

The app works completely offline and does not require any internet connection. No data ever leaves your device.

Permissions

Android Permissions

  • Storage Access (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, READ_MEDIA_*): Required to read files you choose to encrypt or index
  • No other permissions: We don't request location, camera, contacts, or any other permissions

All file access is user-initiated. The app only accesses files that you explicitly select.

Third-Party Services

Docurypt does not use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No crash reporting services
  • No advertising networks
  • No tracking tools
  • No social media integrations
  • No cloud storage services

Data Security

  • AES-256-GCM encryption: Military-grade encryption for vault files
  • Argon2id key derivation: Memory-hard, GPU-resistant password hashing
  • Unique salt per file: Each encrypted file has its own unique salt
  • SHA-256 integrity verification: Ensures files haven't been tampered with
  • Passwords never stored: Your master password is never saved anywhere
  • Local storage only: Everything stays on your device

User Control

  • Add files manually
  • Delete files anytime
  • Reset vault
  • Uninstall app to remove all data

Data Sharing

We do not share any data because we don't collect any data.

Children's Privacy

Docurypt does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Data Retention

All data remains on your device until you delete it, reset the vault, or uninstall the app.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there is no data to access, correct, delete, or export.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date.
